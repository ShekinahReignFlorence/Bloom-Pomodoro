## ✨ Features

- 🌸 **Blooming flower** — a petal grows each time you complete a focus session (8 petals total)
- ⏱ **Animated progress ring** — wraps around the flower as your session counts down
- 🌿 **Gentle sway animation** — the flower sways while the timer is running
- 🔔 **Soft chime sound** — a pleasant ascending tone plays when a session ends
- 🎨 **Break mode** — switches to a calming blue theme during break time
- 📍 **Petal tracker** — 8 small dot indicators track your completed sessions

## 🛠 How to Use

1. Choose your focus duration (25, 45, or 90 minutes)
2. Click **Start** to begin your session
3. Stay focused — the flower will sway while the timer runs
4. When the session ends, a petal blooms 🌸
5. Take your break, then repeat — grow your flower to full bloom!

## 🧠 The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method:

1. Work for a focused session (traditionally 25 minutes)
2. Take a short break (5 minutes)
3. After 4 sessions, take a longer break (15–30 minutes)
4. Repeat

Bloom makes this ritual feel calming and rewarding by turning each session into a petal.
